  \bi

    \item May 2015: JMC meeting: JTC presented MSE simulation results looking at age-1 index and age 2+ survey precision
    \item U.S. fisheries started in May with decent catches
    \item Canadian fisheries
    \item Return of at-sea fleet in the U.S. saw low catch-rates

    \item Survey (exploration) for spawning hake started in January aboard the Shimada
  \ei
